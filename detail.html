DESCRIPTION
Radisson Blu has roots dating back to the opening of the SAS Royal Hotel in Denmark in 1960. Designed by Arne Jacobsen for SAS Group, it was the world's first designer hotel.[1]: 378–379  The hotel was initially under the catering division of the group but merged with the hospitality division to become SAS Catering and Hotels.[1]: 379  In 1982, the hotels were spun off as a separate division, operating under the name SAS International Hotels.[2] and became known as SAS.
